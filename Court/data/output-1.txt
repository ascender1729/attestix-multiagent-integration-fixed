│                                                                                                                            │
│  **Recommendation:** Revise the IP clause to narrow its scope, ensuring that it only applies to inventions, discoveries,   │

│                                                                                                                            │

│  and prevent the employee from using confidential information to compete with the company. However, the employee may       │
│  counter that the clause is overly restrictive and could prevent them from seeking new employment opportunities.           │
│                                                                                                                            │
│  **Risk:** If the employee is found to have engaged in competitive activity during the restricted period, the company may  │
│  be unable to enforce the non-compete clause due to its excessive duration. This could lead to the employee being able to  │
│  compete with the company without penalty.                                                                                 │
│                                                                                                                            │
│  **Recommendation:** Revise the non-compete clause to limit its duration to one year, unless the employee possesses        │
│  highly proprietary trade secrets that directly threaten the core business model.                                          │
